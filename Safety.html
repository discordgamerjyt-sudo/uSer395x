Safety

The files shared here come from Personal Collections, Friends, or Publicly Available Sources . In some cases, contributors may have purchased the material themselves. Most files are briefly checked to ensure they work before being uploaded.

False Positives are not uncommon with cracked softwares and plugins. Most of them can be dismissed by adding the software to your antivirus's exclusion list.

You can use the VirusTotal website to scan any downloaded file for safety.

Remember to check the file behavior before saying anything, Simply just draging the file to virstotal will flag the item because the dll present in the cracked files, "check the behaviour of the file".
